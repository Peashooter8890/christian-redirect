Are You Hungry and Deciding What to Eat?

First off, eat when you are actually hungry so that we may actually feel gratitude to God for his gift of our daily bread.

Now, as for what to eat - the four main categories can be eating at home, eating out with my brother, ordering uber eats, and eating out with friends.
Consider and apply the following algorithm with honesty to determine the best choice of eating:

  1. Eating out with friends for either active engagement in the thought of evangelism or fellowship with churchgoers? Go ahead.

  2. Eating out with my brother? Make sure you have a decent amount of things to talk about - preferably a Christian topic. If not, no.

  3. Not enough rememberance of God? Do some fasting.

  4. Is it dinner and there is nutritious enough food at home? Eat at home.

  5. Is it lunch and there is nutritious enough food at home? Eat at home.

  6. Order uber eats.

If you do eat out with your brother in Dedham, consider the following guidelines:

  1. Choose bamboo, uchi, 29 bistro, eggs & thai for regular occasions of asian eating. Choose chik fil a, five guys, shake shack for general western eating.

  2. Choose outback, noah's kitchen, seoul jangteo, frank pepe, happy lamb, alston ramen, legal seafood, grand india, shanti indian for special occasions.

  3. Choose temazcal, dave's hot chicken, tavern in the square, BBQ Chicken for late night dining options.

  4. Don't eat expensive foods of Cambridge or deeper Boston.

  5. Don't eat breakfast anywhere that's not Tous Les Jours, French Press, or Tatte.

If you order from uber eats, consider the following guidelines:

  1. If you crave sichuan, order twice cooked pork belly from Noah's kitchen. If you crave indian, order Grand India.

  2. Order chik fil a, mcdonalds, five guys, chipotle if you want American food. Only order chipotle if you plan on eating one meal that day.

  3. Order doner kebab from Anatolia if you crave mediterranean. Order frank pepe if you want Pizza.

  4. Order hanmaru buljokbal if you want korean spicy meat and rice for dinner. Order seoulsolongtang or jangteo if you want white broth.

  5. Order genki if you want sushi. Order 29 Bistro Pad Thai if you want Pad Thai.

  6. Consider redwhite boneless ramen?
