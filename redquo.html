Are You Trying to Visit Reddit or Quora?

Keep in mind that reddit and quora doesn't have educational or productive content that aligns with our goal as followers of Christ.

It's best to avoid this platform and use other sources.

  • For entertainment, use the bible and delight in God's word.
  • For ministry oriented coding, use platforms like stackoverflow or general documentation - or even just something like perplexity.
  • For bible study, reddit and quora are probably two of the worst places to go. Use a reputable commentary or academic source.
